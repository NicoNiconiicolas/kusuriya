An ugly tutorial

About this website :

This web application's goal is to boost your flashcards revisions by using the power of motivation obtained through an game like environment and blablabla...

How the game works :


Shop :

Synthetised products will be placed here while waiting to be sold


Catalog :

Upgrade your ingredients quality in order to create products with a better value.


Special items

Some items with particular effects can be bought with money


Exploration :

Here you can review you cards in Kotoba style (write exact answer) or Anki style (correct/wrong answer buttons). Good answers fills the progression bar, when it reaches 100% you'll get a basic ingredient among the 3 existing varieties : berry, blueberry and grape


Management :

Here we have :

1. Today's orders
simply orders for ingredients with a nice payout


2. Weekly bill
You have to pay your bill each before sunday 0:00 UTC


3. Decks management
Here you choose which deck to review
・ one of the JLPT decks coming from Kotoba
・ a custom deck previously saved on your browser
・ through a link redirecting directly to a converted deck hosted on another website

With "New deck" button you can convert any deck exported from Kotoba or Anki PC version (exported in "cards in plain text"). Depending your choice converted deck will be saved in the deck slot of your choice or downloaded in order to be uploaded on another website later
Take care to choose how the spaces will be processed in the answer field, by default spaces are will be considered as separators for multiples answers

4. Reviewing style
Change the reviewing style to get along your tastes or what is more suited for your deck


5. Game's difficulty
Easy : Bill never exceed 3500§ | no taxes | ingredients doesn't rot

Challenge : Each week bill is 50% more than last week's bill | 25% to 75% taxes for a product of each set | Everyday at 0:00 UTC all ingredients rot
